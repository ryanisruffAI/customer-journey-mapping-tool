Tips for Good Problems
  • ✅ Specific: Target a well-defined pain point
  • ✅ Significant: Address a problem people care about solving
  • ✅ Frequent: Occurs regularly enough to matter
  • ✅ Urgent: Creates immediate pressure to solve
  • ✅ Underserved: Current solutions are inadequate
Pro Tip: Focus on problems you personally understand and have experienced. Your domain expertise gives you unique insight!
